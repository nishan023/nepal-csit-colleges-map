BSc CSIT Colleges in Nepal

CSIT College Overview

There are a total of 3036 CSIT seats available across 60 constituent and private colleges. Among these, 2429 seats fall under the Open Quota and 607 seats under the Samabeshi Quota. Specifically, 15 constituent institutions collectively offer 1152 seats (926 Open and 226 Samabeshi), while 45 private institutions account for 1884 seats (1503 Open and 381 Samabeshi).
